About the Society

Five Decades of Sociological
Excellence

Founded in 1975, the UPLB Sociology Society has stood as a pillar of sociological inquiry and social engagement within the University of the Philippines Los Baños. For over half a century, SocSoc has cultivated a community of students dedicated to understanding the intricate fabric of Philippine society.

Rooted in the tradition of critical thinking and Iskolar ng Bayan service, we bridge the gap between academic sociology and real-world social transformation through research, community outreach, and informed public discourse.

Mission

To promote sociological consciousness and foster social responsibility among UPLB students and the wider Los Baños community.

Vision

A society where every individual understands and actively participates in shaping equitable social structures.

Advocacy

Championing human rights, social justice, and inclusive development through evidence-based sociological practice.

Community

Building a vibrant academic community that nurtures critical discourse, mutual respect, and collective empowerment.

Our Legacy
A Half-Century of
Impact
Tracing the milestones that have defined our journey from a small student group to one of the most respected organizations at UPLB.
1975

The Founding

A group of visionary sociology students at UPLB established the Sociology Society, creating a formal platform for sociological discourse and community engagement during a pivotal era in Philippine history.

1986

Post-EDSA Renaissance

In the wake of the People Power Revolution, SocSoc redefined its mission, centering social justice and democratic participation as core organizational pillars and expanding community outreach programs.

1998

First Husay ng Soksay

The inaugural Husay ng Soksay (Excellence in Sociological Inquiry) was launched as the society's flagship academic event, becoming a cornerstone of sociological scholarship at UPLB.

2008

Community Partnerships

SocSoc formalized partnerships with local government units and NGOs in Laguna, establishing sustained community development programs that applied sociological research to real-world challenges.

2018

Digital Transformation

Embracing the digital age, the society expanded its advocacy through social media platforms and digital publications, reaching thousands of students and community members across the Philippines.

2023

4th Husay ng Soksay

The fourth installment of the flagship conference drew participants from universities nationwide, solidifying its reputation as a premier platform for emerging sociological thought in the Philippines.

2025

Golden Jubilee

The UPLB Sociology Society celebrates fifty years of excellence, marking a half-century of nurturing sociological minds and championing social transformation in the Philippines.

How to Join
Become a Member
Joining the UPLB Sociology Society is your gateway to a community of critical thinkers, change-makers, and future sociologists.
01

Express Interest

Attend any of our open events or visit our booth during the university organization fair. Introduce yourself and learn about what SocSoc offers.

02

Submit Application

Complete the membership application form, available at our events or through our social media pages. Provide basic academic information and a brief statement of interest.

03

Attend Orientation

Participate in the mandatory membership orientation where you'll learn about the society's history, mission, organizational structure, and member expectations.

04

Welcome to SocSoc

Upon completion of the orientation, you become an official member with full access to all SocSoc programs, resources, and the vibrant community of sociologists-in-training.

Requirements

  • Must be a bonafide student of UPLB
  • Open to all degree programs (not limited to Sociology majors)
  • Willingness to participate in organizational activities
  • Commitment to the society's mission and values
  • Completion of the membership orientation program

Member Benefits

  • Access to exclusive academic workshops and seminars
  • Priority registration for Husay ng Soksay and flagship events
  • Peer and alumni mentorship network
  • Community engagement and service-learning opportunities
  • Certificate of membership and participation in all events
  • Lifelong membership in the SocSoc alumni network
